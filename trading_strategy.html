Day Trading (Option Index Trading) Plan Setup

  • Step 1: Check VWAP line status for bullish or bearish trend on the Dhan Platform index chart.
  • Step 2: Check ALMA (5,20) crossover for bearish or bullish confirmation before executing trade.
  • Step 3: Check MACD status to confirm crossover movement above or below the mid-line.
  • Step 4: Verify RSI levels: Bullish above 60, Bearish below 40, while also monitoring the 50 mid-line.
  • Step 5: Check VWAP on option premium chart along with crossover, MACD, and RSI confirmation.
  • Step 6: Use Heikin Ashi & Renko charts while trading for stronger buy and sell confirmation.
