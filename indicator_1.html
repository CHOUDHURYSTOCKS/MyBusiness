Detailed Indicator Guide

1. Ichimoku Cloud

A complete trading system combining trend direction, support/resistance, momentum, and future projection.

  • Price above cloud → Bullish
  • Price below cloud → Bearish
  • Cloud thickness → Strength / volatility
  • Tenkan/Kijun crossover → Entry signal
  • Best in trending markets
  • Excellent for swing traders

2. Bollinger Bands

Measures market volatility around a moving average. Bands expand during volatility and contract during quiet markets.

  • Upper band touch → Potential overbought
  • Lower band touch → Potential oversold
  • Band squeeze → Possible breakout
  • Best for ranging markets
  • Popular for mean reversion trading

3. MACD

Momentum indicator based on moving averages that helps traders identify trend shifts.

  • Signal crossover → Trend change
  • Histogram growth → Momentum strengthening
  • Divergence → Possible reversal
  • Best in medium-to-strong trends
  • Excellent for confirmation trading

4. RSI

Measures the speed and strength of price movement to identify overbought and oversold conditions.

  • Above 70 → Overbought
  • Below 30 → Oversold
  • Divergence → Reversal warning
  • Works well in sideways markets
  • Great for beginners & day traders
