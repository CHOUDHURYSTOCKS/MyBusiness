Advanced Institutional Trading Indicator

Volume Profile Indicator

The Volume Profile is one of the most powerful professional trading tools that reveals where major trading activity occurred at specific price levels — helping traders identify institutional zones, support/resistance, fair value, and breakout regions.

Core Idea

Traditional volume indicators focus on time, while Volume Profile focuses on price levels where the market traded the most.

Traditional Volume

“How much volume happened during this candle?”

  • Volume shown per time interval
  • Uses candle-based volume bars
  • Limited price-level insight

Volume Profile

“At which price levels did the most trading happen?”

  • Shows volume at price levels
  • Reveals institutional activity
  • Identifies support/resistance zones

Main Components

1. POC (Point of Control)

Meaning

The price level with the highest traded volume.

Importance
  • Strong magnet price
  • Major support/resistance
  • Institutional activity zone
Interpretation
  • Price above POC → Bullish bias
  • Price below POC → Bearish bias

2. Value Area (VA)

The range where approximately 70% of trading volume occurred.

Components
  • VAH = Value Area High
  • VAL = Value Area Low
Trading Logic
  • Above VAH → Bullish strength
  • Below VAL → Bearish weakness
  • Inside VA → Consolidation

3. HVN (High Volume Node)

Meaning

Price areas with heavy trading activity.

Significance
  • Strong acceptance zone
  • Consolidation region
  • Market comfort area
Behavior

Price often slows down or reverses near HVNs.

4. LVN (Low Volume Node)

Meaning

Areas with very low traded volume.

Significance
  • Rejection zone
  • Fast-moving price area
  • Breakout region
Behavior

Price moves rapidly through LVNs.

How Traders Use Volume Profile

Support & Resistance

  • High-volume areas become strong support/resistance
  • Low-volume zones become breakout regions

Trend Confirmation

  • Above POC & VAH → Bullish
  • Below VAL & POC → Bearish

Breakout Trading

  • Price escapes value area
  • Strong move through LVN

Reversal Trading

  • Near HVN
  • Near previous POC
  • At VAH/VAL extremes

Beginner Setup Suggestion

If you're starting with Volume Profile, keep your setup simple and focus on the most important levels first.

  • Use Visible Range Volume Profile (VRVP)
  • Add VWAP
  • Add 20 EMA
  • Combine with Price Action

Focus Mainly On:

  • POC (Point of Control)
  • VAH (Value Area High)
  • VAL (Value Area Low)
  • LVNs (Low Volume Nodes)

These four alone can significantly improve trade quality and decision making.
